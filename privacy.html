Privacy Policy for VED Usage Logger / Optional Self-Check Tool Last Updated: March 30, 2026 This Privacy Policy describes how the VED Usage Logger ("App") and the associated Optional Self-Check Tool ("Tool") handles your information. 1. Data Collection and Storage The app is designed with a "Privacy First" approach. • Local Storage Only: All data entered into the app—including session logs, wellness scores (Performance Confidence, Satisfaction Level, Firmness Level), measurements (length/circumference), and your name—is stored exclusively on your device's local storage (Local Storage). • Volatile Self-Check Data: Data entered into the Optional Self-Check Tool is processed entirely in your device's temporary memory (RAM). It is not saved to the device's permanent storage and is cleared automatically when the browser tab or session is closed. • No Server Transmission: We do not operate any external servers. Your data is never transmitted, uploaded, or shared with us or any third party. • No Personal Identifiers: We do not collect your email address, phone number, or device ID. 2. Permissions The App may request the following permissions for specific functional reasons: • Storage/Files: Required only to allow you to save your exported PDF reports and CSV data to your device's storage. 3. Third-Party Services • No Analytics: We do not use third-party tracking tools (like Google Analytics or Facebook Pixel). • No Advertising: The App does not contain advertisements. • Integrated Tools: The App provides a link to the Optional Self-Check Tool. Both the App and the Tool operate under this same Privacy-First architecture; no data is shared between them automatically. Data only moves between tools if the user manually uses the 'Copy' and 'Paste' functions. 4. Data Retention and Deletion Since all data is stored on your device, you have full control over it: • Manual Deletion: You can use the "Rest & Archive Data" button within the App settings to permanently erase all stored information. • Uninstalling: Deleting the App from your device will also remove the data stored within the App's local storage. 5. Children’s Privacy The App is intended for adult use. Because we do not collect any data, we do not knowingly collect or maintain information from children. 6. Disclaimer This App is a VED usage data logger and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis and treatment. Always seek the advice of a physician or other qualified health provider with any questions regarding a medical condition.
